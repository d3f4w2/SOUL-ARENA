# Battle Explainability

## Goal
Prevent the fight from feeling like a black box.

## Must Have
- pre-battle equipment preview hints
- weakness-hit messages
- defense/counter messages
- result highlights:
  - 最强一击
  - 最佳反驳
  - 致命漏洞

## Acceptance
- Every major HP or score shift has a textual reason.
- The result page exposes three shareable highlights.
